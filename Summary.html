Summary

Team Natsu consists of Natsu Dragneel and Happy, initially. In the first episode, the duo meet Lucy Heartfilia, an avid fan of the Fairy Tail guild. Eventually the size of the team increases. Together, the team fights strong villians that are wrecking havoc on Mongolia, the setting of the anime or threatening the guild. The power of friendships overcomes the plights the team before their foes. The main antagonists of the show is Zeref Dragneel and Acnologia. Before the final fights with Zeref and Acnologia, the Fairy Tail guild had interesting and explosively eventful missions. They went to Edolas, an alternate dimension. In this alternate dimension, all the characters had a different version of themselves. The guild even participated in the Grand Magic Games against other magic guilds in an attempt to reclaim their title as the number one guild. Watch Natsu, Happy, and Lucy as they complete missions while protecting their friendship!
