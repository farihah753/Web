Types of Magic


  • Celestial Wizards are wizards that summon Celestial beings from the Celestial World through Celestial Keys. The strongest keys are the twelve zodiac keys, designed after the actual zodiac signs. Lucy Heartfilia is from a family of Celestial Wizards and she has an additional power that allows her to physically retain 10% of the power of the celestial being summoned.

  • Dragon Slayer Magic is the magic that is able to slay dragons. There are many generations of Dragon Slayers. First generation slayers are humans raised by dragons. Second generation slayers are humans that have gained slayer magic through external sources: eating a magic stone. There are many types of Dragon Slayer Magic: Fire Dragon Slayer Magic, Sky Dragon Slayer Magic, Metal Dragon Slayer Magic, and Lightning Slayer Dragon, to name a few. Dragon Slayers are powerful as they are, but they have a transformation that increases their power threshold. This higher level power is called Dragon Force.

    • What is Magic?

    Magic comes from naturally occurring mana in the air. Mages are able to harness the mana and use it as magic. The ability to convert mana is hereditary or can be achieved through artificial means. How one harnesses mana, as lightining or fire, is determined by environmental and/or genetic factors. There is forbidden magic which is called Dark Magic. Experimenting with Dark Magic will paint the soul with dark colors. The severity of the Dark Magic can cuase curses such as the Ankhseram. The Ankhseram Curse is the curse of Contradiction. The more one loves life, the more it will kill it. The cursed person will also be immortal.
