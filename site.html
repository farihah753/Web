What is Fairy Tail?

Fairy Tail is an anime, created by Hiro Mashima, that released its first episode on October 12, 2009.The actual manga was serialized in the August of 2006.The plot involves a group of mages in a guild who form a family-like bond. In order to protect their guild, or family, they defeat villains plotting against them. The name of the guild is Fairy Tail.The theme of the anime is the power of friendship. In the guild, mages forms teams for better success at missions. The main team of the show is Team Natsu. Team Natsu has strong, loyal, entertaining members. The team fights enemies of all calibers of prowess. Obviously, as the anime progresses, the difficulty of challenging the enemies worsens. However, they triumph at the end.
